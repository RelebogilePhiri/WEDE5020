About Us

History: Founded in 2024, Phiri's Whiskful Treats began as a family-run kitchen project supplying cupcakes and pastries to loved ones. It has since grown into a bakery specializing in cakes, cookies, artisanal bread, and desserts.

Mission: To create moments of happiness by serving freshly baked goods made with love, creativity, and passion.

Vision: To become the most loved bakery in South Africa through great recipes, friendly service, and community spirit.

Who We Serve: Families, couples, students, tourists, cafés, event planners, and businesses looking for catering and sweet treats.

Our Team: We’re a family of dreamers, bakers, and chocolate lovers. Each team member adds a sprinkle of joy, a dash of creativity, and a whole lot of love into every treat!
